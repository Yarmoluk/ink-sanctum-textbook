# Concept Taxonomy

This document defines the categorical taxonomy for organizing the 200 concepts in the Ink Sanctum Tattoo Aftercare & Preparation Professional Guide learning graph.

## Taxonomy Categories

| Category Name | TaxonomyID | Description |
|---------------|------------|-------------|
| Foundation Concepts | FOUND | Core foundational knowledge including skin anatomy, wound healing basics, and fundamental aftercare principles |
| Healing Phases | HEAL | Concepts related to the healing timeline, stages of wound repair, and healing milestones |
| Hygiene & Prevention | HYG | Infection prevention, hygiene practices, handwashing, and contamination avoidance |
| Washing Protocols | WASH | Proper washing techniques, soap selection, water temperature, and cleansing frequency |
| Moisturizing Care | MOIST | Moisturizing methods, product application techniques, and hydration protocols |
| Product Knowledge | PROD | Aftercare products, ingredients, product types, and selection criteria |
| Healing Signs | SIGN | Normal and abnormal healing indicators, warning signs, and symptom recognition |
| Activity Restrictions | REST | Prohibited activities, restrictions during healing, and lifestyle modifications |
| Pre-Session Prep | PREP | Pre-appointment preparation including hydration, nutrition, skin prep, and readiness |
| Pain Management | PAIN | Pain expectations, tolerance factors, coping techniques, and comfort strategies |
| Health Factors | HLTH | Medical conditions, medications, health screening, and contraindications |
| Long-Term Care | LONG | Ongoing maintenance, UV protection, touch-ups, and lifetime tattoo preservation |

## Category Descriptions

### FOUND - Foundation Concepts
Core building blocks of tattoo aftercare knowledge. These concepts establish the fundamental understanding of skin structure, wound healing mechanisms, and the basic principles that all other concepts build upon.

**Example concepts:** Tattoo Aftercare, Wound Healing, Skin Anatomy, Epidermis, Dermis, Tattoo Ink Placement

### HEAL - Healing Phases
Concepts focused on the chronological progression of tattoo healing. Covers the biological stages of wound repair and the weekly protocols that guide the healing journey from fresh tattoo to fully healed artwork.

**Example concepts:** Healing Timeline, Surface Healing Phase, Deep Healing Phase, Inflammation Stage, Proliferation Stage, Week One Protocol

### HYG - Hygiene & Prevention
Infection prevention strategies and hygiene best practices. These concepts address how to minimize contamination risk and maintain cleanliness throughout the healing process.

**Example concepts:** Infection Prevention, Bacterial Contamination, Hygiene Practices, Handwashing Technique, Clean Environment

### WASH - Washing Protocols
Specific techniques and guidelines for cleaning fresh tattoos. Covers the mechanics of proper washing, timing, frequency, and potential risks of improper cleansing.

**Example concepts:** Washing Techniques, First Wash Timing, Water Temperature, Gentle Cleansing Motion, Pat Drying Method

### MOIST - Moisturizing Care
Methods and principles for keeping tattooed skin properly hydrated during healing. Addresses application techniques, frequency, and the balance between under and over-moisturizing.

**Example concepts:** Moisturizing Techniques, Ointment Application, Thin Layer Method, Dry Healing Method, Wet Healing Method

### PROD - Product Knowledge
Comprehensive information about aftercare products. Covers specific product options, ingredient analysis, product categories, and how to select appropriate products for individual needs.

**Example concepts:** Aftercare Products, Aquaphor, Hustle Butter, Product Ingredients, Fragrance Avoidance, Natural Alternatives

### SIGN - Healing Signs
Recognition and interpretation of healing indicators. Distinguishes between normal healing progression and warning signs that may indicate complications requiring attention.

**Example concepts:** Normal Healing Signs, Abnormal Healing Signs, Peeling Phase, Infection Symptoms, Allergic Reaction Signs

### REST - Activity Restrictions
Activities to avoid during the healing period. Covers water exposure restrictions, sun protection, physical activity modifications, and behaviors that could compromise healing.

**Example concepts:** Prohibited Activities, Swimming Restriction, Direct Sun Exposure, Scratching Prevention, Exercise During Healing

### PREP - Pre-Session Prep
Everything needed to prepare for a tattoo appointment. Covers hydration, nutrition, skin preparation, clothing choices, and mental/physical readiness.

**Example concepts:** Pre-Appointment Prep, Hydration Importance, Nutrition for Healing, Skin Preparation, Clothing Strategies

### PAIN - Pain Management
Strategies for managing discomfort before, during, and after tattoo sessions. Covers pain expectations, tolerance factors, coping mechanisms, and pain relief options.

**Example concepts:** Pain Management, Pain Expectation, Numbing Creams, Breathing Techniques, Pain by Body Area

### HLTH - Health Factors
Medical considerations that affect tattoo timing and healing. Covers health conditions, medications, allergies, and screening factors that determine session readiness.

**Example concepts:** Health Screening, Medical Conditions, Diabetes Considerations, Medication Effects, Allergy Disclosure

### LONG - Long-Term Care
Ongoing maintenance for healed tattoos. Covers sun protection, moisturizing routines, touch-up planning, and strategies for preserving tattoo quality over time.

**Example concepts:** Long-Term Maintenance, UV Protection, Sunscreen for Tattoos, Touch-Up Planning, Color Longevity
